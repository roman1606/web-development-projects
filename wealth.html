The American Dream

Work hard and become Wealthy?

Maby so, but work alone might not cut it. The following graphs[1] show various paths to prosperity.

Education

More advanced schooling helps. If you can avoid the crushing debt of student loans, all the better. Try to find a major that you're good at and that is good to you.

Birthright

For accruing wealth, what's even better then education? Being born wealthy. In the graph below, note how the birthright high enders are able to become more wealthy than the education high enders. So before being born, shop around.

Although the graphs' data is fabricate, the trends are real. See Lisa Keister's "Getting Rich: America's New Rich and How They Got That Way" and Pew Charitable Trusts' "Economic Mobility Project."
